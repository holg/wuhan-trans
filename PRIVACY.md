# Privacy Policy — VoiceTranslate

**Last updated: April 2, 2026**

## Summary

VoiceTranslate processes all data on your device. No data is collected, stored, or transmitted to any server.

## Data Processing

- **Voice recordings** are processed entirely on-device using on-device speech recognition (Apple Speech, WhisperKit, or Cohere Transcribe). Recordings are not stored after transcription.
- **Translations** are performed on-device using the Apple Translation framework. No text is sent to external services.
- **Text-to-speech** uses the built-in AVSpeechSynthesizer. No data leaves your device.

## Paired Device Mode

When using paired device mode (MultipeerConnectivity), translated text is sent directly between devices over local WiFi Direct or Bluetooth. No data passes through any server.

## Apple Watch

Audio recorded on the Apple Watch is sent to the paired iPhone over WatchConnectivity for on-device processing. No data leaves the device pair.

## Model Downloads

ASR models (WhisperKit, Cohere) are downloaded from HuggingFace on first use. This is a one-time download. No user data is sent during this process.

## Data Collection

VoiceTranslate collects **no data whatsoever**. No analytics, no crash reports, no usage tracking, no personal information.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, open an issue at https://github.com/holg/wuhan-trans
